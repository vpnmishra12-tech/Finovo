
# SmartKharcha AI - Free Installation & Sharing Guide 📱

Your app is ready! Here is how you can use it and share it with the world for free.

## 🔗 How to get your Public Link

The link you see in your browser right now is a **Private Preview Link**. It only works for you. To get a link for everyone else:

1. Click the **"Deploy"** button in the Firebase Studio sidebar.
2. Wait for the deployment to finish.
3. You will receive a **Production URL** (like `https://your-app.web.app`). **This is your real app link!**

## 🚀 How to Install as an App (100% FREE)

You don't need Play Store or APKs. Use the **PWA (Progressive Web App)** method for the same experience.

### For Android (Chrome):
1. Open your **Production URL** in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **three dots (menu)** in the top right.
3. Select **"Install App"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**.

### For iPhone (Safari):
1. Open your **Production URL** in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share button** (square with an up arrow) at the bottom.
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.

## 🛠 Critical Setup (One-time)
After deploying, you **must** do this for login to work:
1. Go to [Firebase Console](https://console.firebase.google.com/).
2. Select your project.
3. Go to **Build > Authentication > Settings > Authorized Domains**.
4. Add your **Production Domain** (e.g., `your-app.web.app`) and your **Preview Domain**.
5. Enable **Google Sign-In** in the **Sign-in method** tab.
