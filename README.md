
# SmartKharcha AI - Free Installation & Sharing Guide 📱

Your app is ready! Here is how you can use it and share it.

## 🔗 Where is my Link?

1. **Development Link (Private):** The URL in your browser's address bar right now is your private preview link. You can use this to test the app on your own device.
2. **Production Link (Public):** To get a link that works for everyone (like `https://your-app.web.app`), you need to click the **"Deploy"** button in the Firebase Studio interface.

## 🚀 How to Install as an App (Free)

Since Play Store requires a $25 fee, use the **PWA method** which is **100% FREE** and gives you the exact same experience.

### For Android (Chrome):
1. Open your app URL in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **three dots (menu)** in the top right.
3. Select **"Install App"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**.

### For iPhone (Safari):
1. Open your app URL in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share button** (square with an up arrow) at the bottom.
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.

## 🛠 Next Steps
1. **Authorize Domain:** Go to Firebase Console > Auth > Settings > Authorized Domains and add your preview domain.
2. **Enable Google Login:** Ensure Google is enabled as a sign-in provider in Firebase Console.
